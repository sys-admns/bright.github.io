About Me

I am a PhD student in Space Cybersecurity with research focused on EMP threats to space-dependent infrastructure. My academic journey is driven by a mission to secure future space technologies through evidence-based practices.

Research Focus

My dissertation explores the cybersecurity implications of electromagnetic pulse (EMP) attacks on satellite systems. I integrate mixed-method approaches and work with professionals across cybersecurity and aerospace domains.
